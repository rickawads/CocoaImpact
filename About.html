More About Us

CocoaImpact was founded in 2020 by Ericka Wadleigh. Ericka has worked in the chocolate industry and seen the carelessness put in place by chocolate companies. She also knows how difficult it can be to find the facts about where chocolate comes from. That's why she put together a team to create a one stop website to help inform consumers to make the best purchasing decisions.

CocoaImpact is a non-profit, and we entirely rely on donations to keep our site running. Any excess income to our project is donated directly to charities working to abolish slavery in the chocolate industy.

For specific inqueries, please visit our Contact Us page, thank you!
